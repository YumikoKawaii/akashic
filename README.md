## About

> *"The Akashic Records — said to contain all knowledge of every soul, across all time..."*

Welcome to **Akashic**!

A personal quiz and test management system for people who take learning seriously. Build your own question banks, organize them however you think, and generate tests tailored exactly to what you want to practice. No noise, no gamification — just you and your material.

Think of it as your own library of knowledge, ready to test you on demand.

---

### Banks — Your Archives

Each bank is an independent question archive — keep your English vocabulary separate from your Japanese grammar, your history facts separate from your coding interview prep. Every bank has its own categories, difficulty tiers, and default test configuration.

Share banks with collaborators as editors or viewers. Legacy banks are open to all.

### Test Generation — The Oracle

Specify exactly how many easy, medium, and hard questions you want. Filter by category, question type, or tags. Akashic selects them for you — and backfills intelligently when a difficulty bucket runs short, pulling from adjacent tiers within the same filtered scope.

Answer MCQ options are shuffled on every attempt. No muscle memory shortcuts.
